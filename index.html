About the Book

The Intelligent Enterprise is an executive's playbook for leveraging AI and data to drive real business results. The authors cut through the hype to reveal how companies can become truly data-driven and AI-enabled. You'll discover proven frameworks to integrate AI into your strategy, case studies of enterprises that unlocked new value streams, and actionable insights to stay ahead in the digital age.

  • Frameworks for AI success: Learn step-by-step how to identify high-value AI opportunities and implement them for ROI.
  • Real-world case studies: See how leading organizations in various industries transformed operations with AI and data.
  • Execution playbook: Get guidance on building data-driven teams, measuring impact, and scaling AI initiatives responsibly.

Whether you're a CEO, CIO, or business leader, this guide will equip you with the knowledge to turn emerging technology into competitive advantage. The future of AI is here this book helps you make the most of it.

About the Authors

Jason Goth

Global Chief Technology Officer, Credera

Jason Goth is the global Chief Technology Officer at Credera. With over 30 years of experience in technology consulting and software system design, he has led transformative projects for over two dozen Fortune 100 companies, such as American Airlines and Verizon. Jason is passionate about aligning technology with business goals to create impactful solutions. Outside work, he enjoys fishing and cherishes time spent with his wife, Leslie, their four children, and grandchildren.

Vincent Yates

Global Chief Data Scientist, Credera

Vincent began his career with a strong academic foundation, attending a Ph.D. program in Statistics from UC Berkeley, and holds dual bachelor's degrees in physics and mathematics. He honed his expertise at Microsoft, Uber, and Zillow Group, spearheading AI initiatives for novel products like Scheduled Rides. Before Credera, Vincent served as Global Chief Data Scientist at General Electric, focusing on Industrial IoT applications. Vincent was named the "2024 Most Innovative Leader in Artificial Intelligence" and received recognition as one of the "Most Prominent Chief Data Leaders." Vincent has earned several patents in artificial intelligence, demonstrating his commitment to advancing the field through cutting-edge research and practical applications.
